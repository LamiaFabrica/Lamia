Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need both Discord and Patreon?

Yes! We require both Discord membership and Patreon support (above basic tier) to qualify for a license. This ensures you're part of our community and supporting development.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Your license remains valid until the end of your billing cycle. Renewal happens automatically each month while you remain a Patreon supporter.

What about source code access?

Champion tier ($10+/month) and above receive full source code access via GitHub. You'll be added as a collaborator to our private repositories.

How many devices can I use?

Supporter tier: 3 devices. Champion tier: 3 devices. Enterprise tier: Unlimited. You can deactivate devices anytime to swap them out.

What if I stop supporting?

Your license will expire at the end of your billing cycle. You can re-subscribe anytime to reactivate your license with the same license key.
